Getting Started

Take full advantage of our Finances dashboard by synchronising your Xero data with Jeeve Analytics.

To start the sync process, simply click the “Connect to Xero” button located at the top of the Finances dashboard.

Xero will ask you to authorise Jeeve Analytics to access your account. Login to your Xero account, select your organisation and click allow access.

Once connected, your Xero data will be available for display in your Finance Jeeve Analytics dashboard within 24 hours.

Our Xero integration is a one-way synchronisation. We use data from your profit/loss report, expenses and categories to improve your analytics experience and show you how your dental practice is performing compared to industry standards.

FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Jeeve Analytics to Xero?

Please see our “Getting Started” section at the top of this page. You will be sync’ing with Xero in no time!

Why do I need to connect Jeeve Analytics to Xero?

Jeeve Analytics synchronises information from your profit/loss reports, categories and invoices. We use this information with our custom designed algorithms to give you an overall snapshot of your practice’s health. By integrating with Xero, we can provide accurate analysis of how your practice is really performing.

When and how does the Xero data sync occur?

Data synchronisation from Xero will occur once daily. This happens automatically in the background – you do not need to do anything to synchronise your Xero data with Jeeve Analytics.

My Xero data isn’t updating – what should I do?

If you are experiencing an issue with your Xero data, please contact the Jeeve Analytics support team.

How do I disconnect from Xero?

You can disconnect from Xero by selecting “Clinics” on the left hand menu, then selecting ‘settings’ on the clinic you would like to disconnect. Click the “Disconnect from Xero” button. Note that by doing this we will be unable to synchronise your data from Xero and some Jeeve Analytics charts will be unavailable.
