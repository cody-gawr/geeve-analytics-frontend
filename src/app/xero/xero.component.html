Why are Jeeve Analytics and Xero the perfect match?

Jeeve Analytics analyses all the key information from your Xero reports automatically, directly from Xero's API to ensure you always have the latest data available.

Our financial dashboards display all the key metrics from your PMS and Xero, offering you a clear snapshot of the health of your practice.

How can I connect Jeeve Analytics to Xero?

Connecting Jeeve Analytics to Xero is simple - you will be reviewing your Practice Management and Accounting data in one portal within a few clicks! Simply click on "Connect To Xero" in our apps settings and login with your Xero credentials to provide authorisation. That's it - you are up and running!
